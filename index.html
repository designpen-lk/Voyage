VOYAGE

A Journey in Every Bite

Project Overview

Voyage is a premium chocolate brand that is developed, aiming to take consumers on a sensory journey of taste. The brand concept revolves around exploring the diverse flavors and rich histories of chocolate from various regions.

Brand Identity

Sophisticated brand identity for Voyage has been crafted, incorporating elements that evoke a sense of richness. The logo features cocoa pods, symbolizing the purity and authenticity. The color palette consists of deep browns, golds, and vibrant accents to reflect the luxurious and exotic nature of the chocolates.

Packaging Design

The packaging for Voyage chocolates was designed to be both eye-catching and informative.
